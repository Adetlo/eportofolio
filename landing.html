Project 3 – IT Centralisation & Standardisation

Successfully migrated core banking infrastructure and critical applications from BNI London Branch to BNI Head Office (Jakarta), ensuring seamless integration and minimal disruption. Collaborated closely with IBM and EY consultants to not only address technical aspects but also ensure compliance with PRA and FCA regulations, while maintaining a seamless user experience despite the shift to the head office. For SD-WAN deployment, worked with third-party providers and the head office to guarantee high-quality, stable connectivity between the head office and BNI London. Achieved zero data loss and a 30% reduction in infrastructure costs.

Project 4 – Office Automation (London Branch)

Developed a robust, web-based financial dashboard for Balance Sheet and P&L, leveraging raw text files from daily EOD. The system is powered by SQL-based ETL processes and a PHP/MySQL backend, providing users with interactive filters for tracking financial growth (day-to-day, MoM, YoY). The dashboard includes detailed sections for Accounting, Business, and Risk Management, offering comprehensive visualizations of key metrics. Integrated automated email functionality ensures daily reports are delivered seamlessly to management, enhancing real-time decision-making and oversight.
